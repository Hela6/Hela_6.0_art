ART ABOUT ME WORDS

I am a queer woman who loves dark fashion and brutalist architecture. Parent of two cats and a few plants, I am also a junior web developer. I sometimes experiment with art through collage. I'm scared but also fascinated by the power of nature and the cosmos, how they can easily destroy us. I'm curious to see how we will evolve to adapt to the environmental and societal changes to come.
